The Story

The long way here

I grew up on a farm in northern New South Wales, raised by my grandmother, never quite fitting the mould. Turns out that was the point.

The path since has not been a straight line, and I would not trade it for one. Teaching English in Shanghai. Building friendships in Nepal. Dancing in front of half a million people in Sri Lanka. More than a decade working across China, Southeast Asia, Central Asia and Hong Kong in one of the world's most demanding industries, usually as the only Westerner in the room.

What I learned along the way is simple: business runs on trust, and trust runs on genuine human connection. Not cold outreach. Not polished pitch decks. Sitting down with people, understanding their world, and meaning it.

Some people are good at business. Some people are good with people. Rarely do you find both in the same person. Rarer still, someone who can walk into a room in Chiang Mai, Shanghai or Sydney and make the right people feel understood. That's what I do.

What I Do

Doors open differently in every culture.
I know which way they swing.

For over two decades I've been building relationships across SE Asia, LATAM, Australia and beyond. I specialise in the work that can't be automated or outsourced: being the person who is already there, already trusted, already fluent in how things actually get done.

Strategic Partnerships

Finding the right people, building real relationships with them, and turning those relationships into outcomes that last. From street level to boardroom, across every culture I've worked in.

Market Entry & Expansion

A deal done right in Bangkok is done very differently to one in Buenos Aires or Brisbane. I help organisations grow across emerging markets without the expensive lessons.

Cross-Cultural Bridge Building

I've navigated complex Chinese corporate environments as a trusted Western bridge and built networks that span continents. When two worlds need to understand each other, that's my room.

Speaking & Brand Representation

Stages, expos, delegations and dinners across Asia and beyond. I represent organisations the way I represent myself: warmly, directly and without a script.

What I Believe
“When I speak my truth,
I feel my power
and live in freedom.”

That's my personal creed, and it took me fifty years to earn it. I believe every person has a purpose, and mine is connection: helping people, organisations and cultures genuinely see each other.

I'm drawn to work that changes lives. Education, conscious business, impact finance, social enterprise. If the work matters, I'm interested. If it's just money moving around, I'm not your guy.

I hold a deep spiritual view of life. I won't preach it at you, but it shapes everything: how I treat people, why I choose the work I choose, and my conviction that a life lived from your own truth beats one lived from anyone else's script.
