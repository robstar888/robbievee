Who He Is

Not a consultant.
A trusted operator.

Robbie Vee is an Australian business development and partnerships professional based in Chiang Mai, Thailand. For over two decades, he has built relationships and opened doors across Southeast Asia, Latin America, Central Asia, Australia and beyond.

Not through cold outreach or polished pitch decks, but through genuine human connection, trust, and deep cultural understanding. He has lived and worked across four continents, learning that the most important currency in global business is not capital - it is credibility earned in person.

"Most people talk about partnerships. Robbie builds them - in rooms, over dinners, across cultures, where deals actually happen."

He is drawn to organisations in conscious business, impact finance, education and social enterprise - work that goes beyond profit and creates something that lasts.
