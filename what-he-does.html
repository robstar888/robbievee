The Approach

Business development the human way.

Robbie works with organisations expanding across emerging markets who need someone on the ground. Not a consultant with a slide deck, but a trusted operator who can open doors, build relationships, and get things moving.

He operates where business is human. He understands how deals really get done across different cultures, and how to navigate the nuances that don't show up in strategy decks. His toolkit is built on relationships, not systems.

"Deals happen in rooms, over dinners, and across cultures. Technology is the tool. The human is the product."
